Our Company

MetricΤrade is a leading company providing trading tools to individuals and financial institutions.
MetricTrade Ltd. was founded in 2000 in Thessaloniki, Greece, under the name ZEUS Internet Applications Ltd. and was renamed MetricTrade Ltd. in 2010.
Since 2001, it has been providing solutions for online trading in stocks and, since 2004, for options and futures.
The early adoption of streamlined operations for portfolios, trades, and market data, along with the total quality of the system, allowed our company to dominate the Greek and Cyprus markets in this area.
In 2003, the company became a data feed vendor for the Athens Stock Exchange in 2006, Cyprus Stock Exchange, in 2015 for the NYSE/NASDAQ Stock Exchanges and in 2017 for the London Stock Exchange.
Within a few years, the company became the largest data feed vendor on the Cyprus and Athens Stock Exchanges in terms of the number of terminals.
More than 15 banks and securities companies use our systems to serve their customers, employees, and their brokers.
55% of Greek brokerage companies, representing 40% of Athex Exchanges turnover, cooperate with MetricTrade.

Payment Methods

Credit/Debit Cards
You can renew your subscription via credit, debit and prepaid cards of Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, American Express and Diners Club.



To update your card information, please visit the account page and log in with your username and password.
All payments made using a card are processed through the electronic payment platform "Nexi e-Commerce" and uses TLS 1.2 encryption with a 128-bit encryption protocol (Secure Sockets Layer - SSL). Encryption is a way of encoding information until it reaches its intended recipient, who will be able to decode it using the appropriate key.

Cancellation / Return Policy
If you have selected automatic recurring billing of your card, you can select "Cancel recurring billing" from the "My subscription" page to stop the automatic billing. Please make sure to cancel at least 10 days before the next charge, as the cancellation process takes a few days to finalize.
We recommend that you use the app trial before purchasing a subscription, as you cannot cancel a non-recurring subscription. You can only add or upgrade products to an active subscription. If there are less than 10 days left until the end or the subscription has expired, you can renew the subscription with any add-remove products.
For any other case you can contact us by email at: support@metrictrade.com.
